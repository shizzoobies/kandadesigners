Success Must Be Measurable

Was the content effective?

Training only matters if it creates change.

Kirkpatrick’s Four Levels measure impact from reaction to real results.

1

Reaction

Did learners find it useful?

2

Learning

Did they gain knowledge or skill?

3

Behavior

Did they apply it on the job?

4

Results

Did it impact outcomes?

From completion to impact

Success is not finishing training. It is what happens because of it.
