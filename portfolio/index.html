Nonprofit Leadership

Project Makeover UF

Transforming elementary schools into spaces where students experience wonder, joy, and the belief that anything is possible.

About Project Makeover

Project Makeover is on a mission to help schools spark wonder, joy, and dreams, for the joy of learning. Volunteers descend on elementary schools for a weekend makeover blitz, transforming tired hallways, classrooms, and outdoor spaces into vibrant, inspiring environments that tell kids: you matter, and your dreams matter too.

Kristina's Role

Kristina serves on the board of Project Makeover UF and works directly with volunteers on the ground during makeover weekends. She helps coordinate the teams that show up to paint murals, build gardens, refresh libraries, and bring color and life to schools that need it most. Her nonprofit project management experience, rooted in real community work, shapes how she approaches every collaboration she takes on.
