About Kristina

Kristina Anderson

Instructional Designer & Photographer

Kristina is an educator and Instructional Designer with over 15 years of experience creating meaningful, measurable learning experiences. Beginning her career in K-12 education and expanding into corporate and higher education, she brings a deep understanding of how adults and students learn.

Her work spans eLearning development, curriculum design, photography, and visual storytelling — always grounded in strategy and purpose. Every project she takes on reflects her belief that good design and good learning go hand in hand.
